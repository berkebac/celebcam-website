Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 22, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to CelebCam. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS application.

Important: CelebCam does not retain, store, or collect any face data or biometric information from the photos you upload. Your photos are processed temporarily by our AI partners solely for celebrity selfie generation purposes and are automatically deleted immediately after processing. No facial recognition data is extracted, stored, or used for any other purpose.

  • Credit balance and purchase history (stored securely in Firebase)
  • Subscription status and expiration dates
  • Support Communications: When you contact us for support, we collect your email address and any information you provide in your messages.

Content You Create:

  • Photos you upload for celebrity selfie generation
  • Generated celebrity selfies (stored temporarily for your convenience)
  • Creation metadata (timestamps, celebrity selected, scene used, model used)
  • Generation history

Technical Information:

  • Device information (iOS version, device model)
  • App usage data and crash reports (via Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Network information for service delivery
  • Purchase transaction data (via RevenueCat and Apple)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our celebrity selfie generation services
  • Process your payments and manage credits
  • Manage subscriptions (weekly and monthly plans)
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support
  • Send important updates about the service
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud
  • Comply with legal obligations

3. Data Storage and Security

Local Storage:

Your credit balance, subscription status, and generation history are stored securely in Firebase Firestore, accessible only to you through your authenticated account.

Cloud Processing:

Images you upload are temporarily processed by our AI partners (including fal.ai) to generate celebrity selfies. These images are automatically deleted immediately after processing. No face data, facial recognition information, or biometric data is retained or stored.

Abuse Prevention:

We may temporarily retain uploaded content for a limited period (up to 72 hours) solely for the purpose of detecting, preventing, and investigating abuse, illegal activity, or violations of our Terms of Service. This data is automatically deleted after this period and is never used for any other purpose.

Generated Content Storage:

Generated celebrity selfies are stored in Firebase Storage for your convenience and can be accessed through the app's history feature. You can delete any generated content at any time through the app.

Security Measures:

We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure API communications (HTTPS)
  • Firebase Authentication with Apple Sign In
  • Firebase Security Rules to protect user data
  • Secure payment processing through Apple and RevenueCat

Each third-party service has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies:
